# **9thform Online Store — Product Requirements Document (PRD)**

## **1. Overview**
The 9thform Online Store is a modern, interactive, action‑sports‑inspired e‑commerce website designed around the aesthetics of the provided brand logos and the visual inspiration reference. The site must feel futuristic, cinematic, and clean while maintaining the raw edge of an action sports apparel brand. It will feature interactive UI elements, smooth animations, and a rotating 3D object on the homepage.

## **2. Goals & Objectives**
- Create a professional and visually striking online store for 9thform.
- Showcase products (hat + shirt) in a minimal but high‑impact layout.
- Integrate interactive UI elements (e.g., rotating top‑of‑page graphic).
- Provide intuitive navigation for shop, about, and contact pages.
- Maintain cohesive branding using provided logos and brand colors.
- Ensure clean mobile responsiveness.

## **3. Target Audience**
- Skate, surf, and action sports communities.
- Young creatives, lifestyle consumers, and streetwear fans.
- Customers familiar with cinematic, nostalgic, and futuristic design aesthetics.

## **4. Brand & Visual Direction**
### **Brand Identity Requirements**
- Use 9thform logos from provided files.
- Aesthetic: cinematic, futuristic, clean, action‑sports‑driven.
- Inspiration from reference site: minimal, bold typography, generous whitespace.
- Color palette pulls from inspo image: grayscale tones, black, white, soft gray.

### **Design Principles**
- Minimal but dynamic.
- Strong typographic hierarchy.
- Grid‑based layouts.
- Subtle hover + scroll animations.
- Smooth transitions between sections.

## **5. Core Pages & Features**

### **5.1 Home Page**
**Hero Section:**
- Rotating 3D logo/object on a central axis at the top of the page.
- Clean background following color palette.
- Large bold text similar to inspiration.

**Scroll Interaction:**
- As the user scrolls, reveal a **SHOP** button.
- Additional branded visuals.
- Smooth animations.

**Footer (global):**
- Links to Instagram, email, Etsy.
- Links to: Privacy Policy, Terms of Service, Shipping Policy, Refund Policy, Contact.

---

### **5.2 Shop Page**
**Product Listings:**
- Hat (Item 1)
- Shirt (Item 2)

Each item includes:
- Product image(s)
- Name & short description
- Price
- Add‑to‑Cart button (if cart functionality is added in future)

Layout: clean grid, large images, minimal text.

---

### **5.3 About Page**
A clean, simple page describing the 9thform brand.

**Content Requirements:**
- Who we are
- What 9thform represents
- Inspiration behind the action‑sports‑driven visuals
- Mission + vision

Design mirrors the inspiration site: bold headings, spaced layout.

---

### **5.4 Contact Page**
**Contact Form Fields:**
- Name
- Email
- Message
- Submit button (styled futuristic + clean)

Page also contains:
- Email link
- Instagram link
- Etsy link

---

## **6. Footer Requirements (Global on All Pages)**
**External Links:**
- Instagram
- Email
- Etsy

**Internal Links:**
- Contact
- Privacy Policy
- Terms of Service
- Shipping Policy
- Refund Policy

Design should be minimal and unobtrusive.

## **7. Navigation/Menu**
Navigation bar includes:
- **Shop**
- **About**
- **Contact**

Clean top navbar, fixed or subtle fade‑in on scroll.

## **8. Interactivity & Animations**
- Rotating 3D object/logo on homepage.
- Hover interactions on product cards.
- Smooth scrolling.
- Animated page transitions.
- Motion similar to action‑sports cinematic feel—clean and not distracting.

## **9. Technical Requirements**
- Fully responsive mobile‑first layout.
- Fast loading times.
- Compatible with modern browsers.
- If using frameworks: React, Next.js, or equivalent.
- Integrate static asset hosting for logos.

## **10. Assets Needed**
- Provided logos (all variants).
- Product images: hat and shirt.
- Text content for About section.
- Brand color values.

## **11. Success Criteria**
- Site feels premium and futuristic.
- Clean, action‑sports‑aligned visuals.
- Easy shopping navigation.
- Strong brand identity consistently applied.

## **12. Timeline**
- Design mockups: 1 week.
- Frontend build: 2–3 weeks.
- Testing & QA: 1 week.
